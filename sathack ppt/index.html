The Problems & Our Solutions

Problem 1: Overcrowding & Security

Limited event space and sharable passes create security risks from outsiders.

Solution: Secure QR Passes

We issue non-shareable, unique QR passes for each event. They are scanned once at the entrance, blocking unauthorized/repeat entries and managing capacity.

Problem 2: Student Safety & Flow

Difficult to track student activity or manage crowd density in real-time.

Solution: Activity Timestamp System

Our website creates a background timestamp of student check-ins (at events, stalls, etc.) to help manage crowd flow and enhance campus safety.
